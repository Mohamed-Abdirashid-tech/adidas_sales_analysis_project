# Adidas Sales Performance Analysis 📊

## Retail Data Analytics Project Using Python

---

## 📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes Adidas retail sales data to uncover key business insights related to revenue performance, profitability, product demand, and regional sales distribution.

The goal is to transform raw sales data into meaningful insights that support data-driven business decisions.

Using Python and data analysis libraries, we explore patterns in sales performance, identify top-performing regions and products, and evaluate profitability across different business dimensions.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

- Clean and preprocess raw sales data
- Perform exploratory data analysis (EDA)
- Analyze regional and city-level performance
- Identify top-performing products
- Study sales trends over time
- Evaluate profitability and margins
- Understand the impact of sales methods
- Generate actionable business insights

## 🧹 Data Cleaning & Preparation

The dataset was cleaned and preprocessed to ensure accuracy and consistency:

- Converted incorrect data types (sales, profit, margin)
- Standardized column names
- Converted invoice date to datetime format
- Removed duplicate records
- Handled missing values
- Created new features:
  - Year
  - Month
  - Month Name
  - Profit Ratio

## 📊 Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)

The analysis focused on:

- Revenue and profit performance
- Regional sales distribution
- Product performance comparison
- Monthly sales trends
- Sales method effectiveness
- City-level performance
- Correlation between business metrics
- Profitability distribution

---

## 📈 Key Insights

- Sales are heavily concentrated in specific regions and cities.
- A small number of products contribute to the majority of revenue.
- There are clear seasonal patterns in sales performance.
- Profitability varies significantly across regions and products.
- Sales channels impact overall revenue distribution.
- Strong positive correlation exists between sales and operating profit.

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations

- Focus on high-performing regions for expansion.
- Optimize low-performing regions with targeted strategies.
- Strengthen marketing for top-selling products.
- Improve underperforming product categories.
- Leverage seasonal trends for inventory planning.
- Optimize sales channels for better profitability.
- Improve pricing strategy to increase profit margins.
